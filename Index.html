Step 1: Tokenization

Before an AI can process text, it splits the sentence into smaller chunks called tokens. Let's use the sentence: "Satyam helps you upskill in AI"

"Satyam helps you upskill in AI"

Step 2: Token Embeddings

AI models convert tokens into mathematical vectors (arrays of numbers) so they can understand relationships. Words with similar meanings have similar vectors.
