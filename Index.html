Step 11: From Attention to LLM

Real transformers stack 96 layers, multi-head attention, feed-forward networks, residuals & positional encoding. Attention formula:

Attention(Q,K,V) = softmax(Q·Kᵀ / √d) × V

Step 12: Reasoning & Chain‑of‑Thought

LLMs predict next token. Chain‑of‑Thought (CoT) breaks reasoning into steps, each step becomes context for the next prediction — easier than one giant leap.

📌 TL;DR: Q and K find matches, V provides content. Learned weights make AI understand context.
